Cohort Analysis Insights:

1. Customer retention is strongest in the first 2–3 months after acquisition.
2. The December 2010 cohort was the largest, but it experienced a steady decline in retention over time.
3. Some smaller cohorts (e.g., early 2011) retained customers slightly better at 3–4 months.
4. Overall, most customers churn within the first 6 months, suggesting the need for better engagement strategies.
5. Retention improvements in later months would significantly impact recurring revenue.
